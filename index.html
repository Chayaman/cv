Chay Vannet

Founder and Photographer of EyeChay.

My greatest passion of a photographer is to create imagery that will stir something in your soul. I believe that imagery can be beneficial to the sick, can encourage the discouraged, and can save the lost. I believe that because it has done that for me.

My love for photography goes hand in hand with my travels. My camera gave me the opportunity to see the unseen for the past 7 years. I have felt the heat of the sun and the love from my roots in #cambodia. I walked on the edge of the highest mountain with the northern people of #thailand in #chiangmai and I have bowed countless times to the humble people of #japan.

I am grateful to my clients who had put their trust in me. And to my friends and family, thank you. Without your guidance and support, this journey may have not been possible.
